Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 15, 2026

Welcome to CoinQuest. We are committed to protecting your privacy, especially for children using our app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information in compliance with Google Play Policies and COPPA (Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act).

1. Target Audience

CoinQuest is designed for children and families. We strictly follow the Google Play Families Policy and ensure a safe, age-appropriate experience.

2. Information We Collect

We DO NOT collect any personal information.

We do not collect:

  • Names, emails, or phone numbers
  • Precise location data
  • Contacts or personal identifiers

We may collect limited non-personal data such as:

  • Device type and OS version
  • App usage behavior (features used)
  • Crash logs and performance data

3. How We Use Information

  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Enhance user experience
  • Fix bugs and optimize features

4. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If any such data is identified, it will be immediately deleted.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party tools such as:

  • Google Firebase (analytics, crash reporting)
  • Google AdMob (only if enabled in future)

These services may collect limited non-personal data under their own privacy policies and in compliance with Google Play requirements.

6. Advertising

If ads are introduced, they will comply with the Google Play Families Policy:

  • No behavioral or interest-based targeting for children
  • Only age-appropriate ads will be shown

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties. Any data is used strictly for improving app functionality.

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect data from unauthorized access, alteration, or misuse.

9. User Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted here with the revised date.
